Carter Beauford is my favorite drummer because of how musical he is. His open style playing (left-hand lead) is the result of watching Buddy Rich on TV at such a young age (two years old) that he didn't realize that when he set the drums up to match Buddy's kit he was actually seeing a mirror image.

Because of this simple "mistake," Carter began a journey of drumming with a unique style of playing that afforded him an ambidextrous ability to easily move around the drums and fludily play beats no matter which hand played which drum.

For those who aren't aware, he is the drummer for the Dave Matthews Band.
